About Mummy's Messy Makers

Nurturing Development Through Play

At Mummy's Messy Makers, we believe that learning happens best when children are having fun. Our sensory play classes are carefully designed to stimulate all five senses, promoting cognitive development, fine motor skills, and creativity.

Led by experienced early childhood educators, our sessions provide a safe, supportive environment where babies and toddlers can explore, discover, and develop essential skills through hands-on play experiences.

Our Classes

Each class is designed to engage specific senses and developmental milestones

Baby Explorers

Gentle sensory experiences including textured play mats, soft materials, musical instruments, and safe taste exploration. Perfect for developing early sensory awareness.

6-12 months

Toddler Adventures

More complex sensory activities including water play, finger painting, playdough, and scented materials. Designed to enhance creativity and fine motor skills.

12-24 months

Little Scientists

Interactive experiments with safe kitchen ingredients, color mixing, texture exploration, and simple cause-and-effect activities.

2-3 years

Creative Mess Makers

Advanced sensory play including themed activities, collaborative projects, and multi-sensory storytelling experiences.

3-4 years
